About Asansol Engineering College

Asansol Engineering College, a self-financing institute, was established in the year 1998. Located in Asansol, Burdwan, West Bengal, the college offers a range of academic programs including B.Tech, MCA, and M.Tech across various disciplines. It holds approval from the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and is affiliated with Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (MAKAUT), formerly known as West Bengal University of Technology (WBUT). Furthermore, the college holds recognition from the University Grants Commission (UGC) under section 2f of the UGC Act, 1956.

About Us


The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) is an American 501(c)(3) professional association for electronics engineering, electrical engineering, and other related disciplines.

The IEEE has a corporate office in New York City and an operations center in Piscataway, New Jersey. The IEEE was formed in 1963 as an amalgamation of the American Institute of Electrical Engineers and the Institute of Radio Engineers.
